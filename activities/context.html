How can The Information School raise awareness of it's existence? (30 min)

Our wonderful space in Bloedel Hall is basically invisible.

  • Pair up
  • Brainstorm solutions for 5 minutes
  • Navigate the building and the grounds
  • Observe it's structure, talk to its inhabitants, and use your surroundings to generate as many ideas as possible.
  • Return in 20 minutes and we'll reflect on how this affected your ideation.

Synthesize (20 min)

Form groups of 3 or 4 and combine your insights and ideas into a design idea you think best solves the problem.

Discuss (10 min)
