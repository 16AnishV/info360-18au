GenderMag walkthrough

A full draft of your solution description is due next week. Before finalizing your design, let's verify both it's usability and inclusiveness with a GenderMag walkthrough.

Plan your evaluation

  1. Pick a persona (e.g. Abby)
  2. Pick a task in your user interface to walk through
  3. Determine the intended sequence of subgoals and actions to achieve that ask in the interface.

Write your task, subgoals, and actions in a shared Google Doc.

Run your walkthrough

  1. Pick the next subgoal for that scenario (e.g. "See bookstore map"), then answer these questions:
    • Would Abby have formed this subgoal is part of her overall goal?
    • Why? Consider Abby's motivations and strategies.
  2. Pick an action for that subgoal (e.g. "Tap 'Browse Off'"), then answer the questions for that action.
    • Would Abby know what to do at this step?
    • Why? Consider Abby's knowledge, skills, motivations, strategies, and self-efficacy.
    • If Abby does the right thing, will she know she's making progress toward her goal?
    • Why? Consider Abby's self-efficacy and attitudes toward risk.
  3. Return to step 2 unless you've done enough actions to complete your subgoal
  4. Return to step 1 unless you've done enough subgoals to complete the task

For each action and subgoal, copy the questions above, and answer them in your Google Doc.

Redesign

  • What should change about your mockups? Write a change list in your Google Doc.
  • What rationale should you capture to justify your final design?

Submit your results

Export a PDF of your results and email them to the instructor for credit. Make sure everyone's name is on the PDF.
