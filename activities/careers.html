What do you want to do?

Andrew J. Ko & Nigini Oliveira

Before we dive into design methods, I think it's important to reflect on how all of these skills and perspectives might be relevant to your goals. In this activity, you'll write some of your goals and we will discuss them in class in light of design careers.

Describe your possible self [Prepare for class!]

On a piece of paper, write me a description of your life in 5 years.

  • Where will you be?
  • What will you be doing?
  • What will be your job?
  • What skills will you have?
  • Where is this degree going to take you?

Think seriously about this. I want to know where you're headed so I can help you get there.

Bring this to class as it will count for your activity points!

Let's discuss design roles in depth

For each role below, I'll explain the role, give some examples of the role in practice, and then we will take on the role for a 5-minute as we worked for Netflix.

  • User experience researchers. Learn about problems in context.
    • Interview as many classmates as you can about their movie and television viewing habits.
  • Data scientists. Learn about problems through data.
    • Study people's movie and television viewing habits by gathering large scale data.
  • Product designers. Meet a market need through design.
    • Envision Netflix product design opportunities that would increase revenue.
  • Interaction/UX designers. Envision and improve a user interface.
    • Critique the Netflix interaction design.
  • Graphic designers. Envision the visual experience of an interface (typography, layout, colors).
    • Critique Netflix visual design.
  • Software engineers. Implement a design
    • Brainstorm how to ensure Netflix movies always start instantly.
