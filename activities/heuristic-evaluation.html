Heuristic evaluation

Requires: paper, pencil or pen

Find a partner and together select a web application that at least one of you visits frequently.

Evaluate (45 min)

Try to find at least two violations of each of the ten heuristics:

  • Visibility of system status
  • Real world match
  • Consistency and standards
  • Error prevention
  • Recognition instead of recall
  • User control and freedom
  • Flexibility and efficiency
  • Diagnose and recover from errors
  • Minimalist design
  • Help and documentation

In a document, capture each violation in the following format

  1. URL or description of page
  2. Description of violation

Reflect (10 min)

  • What was difficult about performing this evaluation?
  • Do you think you found real problems?
  • What methods would have been more effective?

Credit (3 min)

Email your document of violations to the TA for credit. Make sure it has both of your names on it.
