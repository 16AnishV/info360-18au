User research

Your goal in this lab is to:

  1. Identify problems
  2. Select a problem
  3. Begin acquiring expertise on the problem
  4. Begin describing the problem

To achieve this:

  1. Interview your sister team.
  2. With your teammate, discuss the problems you learned about.
  3. Select a problem.
  4. Plan your problem definition draft, due at the beginning of our next class.
    • What's your argument?
    • What research do you need to do to substantiate it?
      • Read research literature about it
      • Do market research on existing solutions to the problem
      • Conduct more interviews and additional user research to understand the problem further
    • What's your plan for drafting a problem statement by the next class?
