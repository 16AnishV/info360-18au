Usability test

A full draft of your solution description is due next week. Before finalizing your design, verify it's usability.

Plan your test

  • What task do you want to verify? Choose the most important ones.
  • What will constitute success on the task?
  • What instructions will you give your participants?

Run your test

  • When you're ready to participate, go to the front
  • When you're ready to run, raise your hand
  • Run at least 5 people through your test.
  • Write down all breakdowns that occur.

Redesign

  • What should change about your mockups? Write a change list.
  • What rationale should you capture to justify your final design?
  • Done with time to spare? Start working on your specification.
