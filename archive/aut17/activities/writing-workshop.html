Writing workshop

By now, you should have a clear idea and mockups of all of the screens for your idea. Today we'll work on writing about your idea, to help convey the behavior of your design in written form. Remember, the goal of your specification is to write the document to a level of detail that an engineer could take your description and build it without having to consult with you on any details.

  • Check out the example specification.
  • Create a shared Google Doc with your teammate. This will be the draft of your design specification.
  • Create an outline of your specification following the guidelines in the project description.
    • Brainstorm what details are in and out of scope.
    • How will you organize the description of your design?
    • What kinds of diagrams or annotations will your mockups need to be clearly understood by your reader?
    • Who is going to write what and by when?
